# Idea

Entry for GameDev.js Game Jam 2026. Theme is `Machine`

Random thoughts:

- Story driven horror game, with an answering machine as the main thing.
    - the player is receiving messages from his ex-girlfriend, driving him insane.
- There has to be some sort of twist at the end, like
    - the player is the killer and reliving his murder. The answering machine is his conscience.
    - the player is the person that was murdered.
    - the player is a police officer investigating a missing person
- Slowly add more and more tapes to the apartment filling the fridge, the bathtub and everywhere.
- Slowly change things in the apartment. Different paintings, blood and dirt on the walls, stuff disappearing.
- Play with sounds (rain, thunder, squicking wood, door slam, breaking glass, knocking), make them positional
- Add Footstep sounds
- Lights flickering (maybe show a ghostly silhouette in the door opening when the lights flicker)
- Protagonist or antagonist proposed and she said no
- Text (in blood) appearing on the wall
 
Horror story tips:
- Somehow make the player love the protagonist. Make it seem he did something good.
- Base on a true story... Where "true" is used very broadly, could be 90% bullshit
- Introduce tension, and then extend it. Maybe the answering machine can tell something the looks like it reveals
  something, but in fact doesn't directly. Maybe something in a room, but nothing happens until much later. This could
  give the player an expectation at some point in the game, but subvert it later, creating a sense of unease and
  unpredictability.
- plan shocking and emotional moments
- plan the ending
